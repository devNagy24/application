Software Developer Internship

Location: Seattle, WA

Duration: 12 Weeks

Job Description

We are looking for a talented and motivated software developer intern to join our team. In this role, you will work closely with experienced developers on various projects and tasks, learning valuable skills and gaining hands-on experience in the software development industry.

The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in computer science, be passionate about technology, and eager to learn and grow as a software developer. If this sounds like you, we would love to hear from you!

Responsibilities

Assist with the development and maintenance of software applications

Primarily focused IOS & Android development

Learn and adapt to new technologies and tools

Participate in code reviews and contribute to the improvement of code quality

Communicate effectively with team members and project stakeholders

About Us

The products we build have a positive impact on people's lives and the world around us. If you are just starting your journey in software development, or if you are a seasoned pro, we have a place for you here. Our apps are used all around the world, so you will have an opportunity here to learn and grow in an environment where millions of people every day interact with our technology.

Our top paid CEOs don't rake in the cash at your expense, and our shareholders only get a dividend. This enables us to pay our employees with the best wages and benefits imaginable. We don't offer merely "competitive" wages, we pay each employee the largest cut of the product of their labor. Also, unlike our competitors, we don't manipulate our users or sell their data to stay in business. Truly a cutting edge company, you will love working here.
